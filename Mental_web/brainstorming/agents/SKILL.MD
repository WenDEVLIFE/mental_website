## Definition of the Topic
The study focuses on the design and development of a Student Mental Health Appointment Support System with integrated self-assessment and scheduling features for PLMUN students. This system aims to provide students with a convenient and accessible platform to evaluate their mental health status, book counseling appointments, and access helpful mental health resources. 

It addresses the growing need for mental health support by offering a digital solution that improves communication between students and counselors while promoting early detection and intervention of mental health concerns.

---

## Objectives of the Study

### General Objectives
To design and develop a web-based system that supports PLMUN students in managing their mental health through self-assessment tools and an efficient appointment scheduling system.

### Specific Objectives
1. **Account Management:** Develop a system that allows students to create and manage their personal accounts securely.
2. **Self-Assessment:** Implement a self-assessment feature that helps students evaluate their mental health condition.
3. **Appointment Scheduling:** Design an appointment scheduling system for booking, rescheduling, and canceling counseling sessions.
4. **Real-time Notifications:** Provide real-time notifications and reminders for scheduled appointments.
5. **Admin Management:** Enable administrators to manage users, appointments, and system data efficiently.
6. **Resource Access:** Provide access to mental health resources such as articles, tips, and guides.
7. **Progress Tracking:** Allow students to track their mental health progress based on previous assessment results.

---

## System Features

### Admin Features
1. **User Management:** Manages all student accounts and system access.
2. **Verification:** Verifies and updates student information when needed.
3. **Assessment Management:** Creates and manages self-assessment questionnaires.
4. **Monitoring:** Reviews and monitors student assessment results.
5. **Counselor Scheduling:** Manages and controls appointment schedules of counselors.
6. **Request Handling:** Approves or declines appointment requests from students.
7. **Counselor Assignment:** Assign available counselors to student appointments.
8. **Schedule Tracking:** Monitors all schedules and completed appointments.
9. **Communication:** Sends announcements, notifications, and reminders to users.
10. **Audit Logs:** Tracks system activities using activity logs.
11. **Maintenance:** Handles system maintenance and updates when necessary.
12. **Data Integrity:** Ensures data accuracy, privacy, and organization in the system.
13. **Content Management:** Manages and updates mental health resources in the system.

### User (Student) Features
1. **Profile Management:** Creates and manages personal account information.
2. **Self-Assessment:** Takes self-assessment tests to evaluate mental health status.
3. **Feedback:** Views assessment results with basic feedback or recommendations.
4. **Booking:** Books, reschedules, or cancels appointments with counselors.
5. **Alerts:** Receives notifications and reminders for upcoming appointments.
6. **History:** Views appointment history and session details.
7. **Resources:** Accesses mental health resources such as tips, articles, or guides.
8. **Progress Tracking:** Tracks personal progress based on previous self-assessments results.
